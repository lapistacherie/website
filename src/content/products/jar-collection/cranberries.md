Jar contains Dried Whole Cranberries.

Cranberries are a rich source of several vitamins and minerals, especially vitamin C and Manganese. Cranberries also have one of the highest contents of antioxidants.

## Consumption and Storage:

These tart gems are more than just a delightful addition to your plate. Cranberries make a great health snack while also elevating your desserts and salads with premium finesse.

Store in a cool and dry place. Refrigerate to extend the shelf life.
